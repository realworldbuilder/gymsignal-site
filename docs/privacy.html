Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

GymSignal is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS app and related services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address (optional, for Pro tier features)
  • App usage preferences and settings
  • Feedback and support communications

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device information (iOS version, device model)
  • App usage analytics (which features you use, crash reports)
  • General location (country/region only, for trend relevance)

Third-Party Data

  • Social media trend data from public APIs
  • Fitness content performance metrics (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

  • Trend Intelligence: We fetch data from our servers to provide you with algorithmic insights and trending content information
  • AI Content Generation: We use OpenAI's API to generate fitness-specific content suggestions based on current trends
  • App Improvement: Usage analytics help us improve features and fix bugs
  • Communication: We may send you app updates or respond to support requests (only if you provide your email)

Data We Don't Collect

  • Personal health or fitness data
  • Your social media account credentials
  • Your personal posts or content
  • Precise location data
  • Contact list or phone information

Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI content generation. Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and payments. Subject to Apple's privacy policy.
  • Analytics Provider: For app usage analytics (we may implement this in future updates).

Data Storage and Security

  • Most data is processed locally on your device
  • Server communications are encrypted (HTTPS)
  • We don't store personal information on our servers unless you explicitly provide it
  • Free tier users can use the app without creating an account

Your Rights

  • You can use the free tier without providing any personal information
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • You can contact us to request deletion of any data we have about you
  • You can opt out of analytics if we implement them in future versions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our app evolves. We'll notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email if you've provided one.

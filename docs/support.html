Frequently Asked Questions

How does GymSignal work?

We monitor multiple data sources daily to track what's trending in fitness content. Our AI analyzes engagement patterns, viral hooks, and algorithmic preferences to give you actionable intelligence on what to post and when to post it.

What's included in the free tier?

Free users get weekly briefings, basic trend alerts, and access to our format cheat sheets. It's everything you need to start improving your content strategy.

What's the difference between free and pro?

Pro ($4.99/mo) unlocks daily briefings, AI content generation, trending hooks, optimal posting times, TikTok→IG pipeline tools, and full access to our trend data. Pro users get real-time intelligence while free users get weekly summaries.

How often is the data updated?

Our trend intelligence is updated daily. Trending hooks are refreshed weekly based on the latest performance data. Hot topic alerts are sent in real-time when we detect emerging trends.

Do I need to connect my social media accounts?

No. GymSignal provides intelligence and suggestions, but you maintain full control over your accounts. We don't need access to your social media profiles to help you grow.

Is this just for fitness influencers?

GymSignal is built for anyone creating fitness content - from personal trainers sharing client transformations to gym owners promoting their facility. If you post fitness content, we can help you get better results.

Which platforms does GymSignal support?

Our intelligence covers TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. We also provide specific tools for adapting TikTok content to Instagram (and vice versa) to maximize your reach across platforms.

Can I cancel my pro subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel through your App Store settings anytime. You'll continue to have pro access until the end of your current billing period, then automatically return to the free tier.

Is my data secure?

We take privacy seriously. We don't collect personal health data or require access to your social accounts. Most processing happens locally on your device. Check our privacy policy for full details.

How is this different from other social media tools?

Most tools show you what was popular last week. GymSignal predicts what's about to trend and gives you specific, fitness-focused intelligence. We're built by creators for creators, not corporate marketers.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Send us your questions, feedback, or feature requests.
